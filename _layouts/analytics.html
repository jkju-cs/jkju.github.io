How to connect Google Analytics

Follow these steps to embed your Google Analytics dashboard:

  1. Go to Google Analytics ↗
  2. Create a new Google Analytics 4 property for your website
  3. Add your GA4 Measurement ID to _config.yml under analytics.google.id
  4. Use Looker Studio ↗ to create an embeddable dashboard
  5. Replace the iframe placeholder below with your Looker Studio embed code

* The mock data shown below will be replaced once your GA is connected.
